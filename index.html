What are Goblins?

In the shadow realms of folklore and fantasy, goblins emerge as malevolent entities, draped in darkenss and mischief. These creatures, with twisted features and malevolent grins, embody the essence of malevolence.

Legends paint goblins as sly tricksters, reveling in chaos, and weaving nefarious plots under the cloak of the night. Their laughter ehcoes through haunted woods, as they delight in spreading fear and uncertainty.
